Healthcare Data Analysis & Patient Insights

Objective: Analyze hospital patient data to uncover insights into treatment costs, patient outcomes, and healthcare performance.

Key Insights:

  • Older patients tend to incur higher treatment costs and report lower satisfaction levels
  • High-cost conditions such as cancer and heart diseases are associated with longer hospital stays
  • Patients who were readmitted reported lower satisfaction scores, indicating gaps in treatment effectiveness
  • Female patients showed higher average treatment costs compared to male patients
  • Critical conditions like heart attacks and strokes are more common among older age groups
Tools: Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
